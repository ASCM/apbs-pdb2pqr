Standard inverse power method for minimum eigenvalue estimation.

Note:
To test, note that the 3d laplacean has min/max eigenvalues:

lambda_min = 6 - 2*dcos(pi/(nx-1))

  • 2*dcos(pi/(ny-1))
  • 2*dcos(pi/(nz-1))

lambda_max = 6 - 2*dcos((nx-2)*pi/(nx-1))

  • 2*dcos((ny-2)*pi/(ny-1))
  • 2*dcos((nz-2)*pi/(nz-1))
